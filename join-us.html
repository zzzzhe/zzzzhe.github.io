Job Title

Senior Speech / Acoustic Algorithm Engineer


Job Description

  1. Follow the latest technology developments in the industry and be able to propose innovative algorithms and models in conjunction with the actual business scenarios of speech.
  2. Responsible for the training and optimization of acoustic models and other related work, deeply involved in the design and development of speech synthesis system modules, including text pre-processing, word separation, lexical, rhyme, semantic understanding and other modules.
  3. Cooperate with product efficiency and stability optimization, and solve the problems of product feedback.

Position Requirements

  1. Master's degree or above in computer science, automation, mathematics or other related fields.
  2. Good understanding of data structures and algorithms, proficiency in C++ and Python.
  3. Familiar with common machine learning and deep learning algorithms, and curious about unknown algorithms.
  4. Familiar with the construction and optimization of general speech synthesis engines, with experience in engine optimization.
  5. Familiarity with or understanding of common models of natural language processing, such as BERT, GPT-2, etc., proficiency in using at least one deep learning framework (PyTorch or Tensorflow), and relevant project experience.
  6. Good team communication skills, excellent mathematical and logical thinking skills, passionate about solving challenging problems, and good at problem solving and analysis.
  7. Experience in speech field is preferred, and published conference papers in speech synthesis, processing and other related fields are preferred.

职位名称

高级语音/声学算法工程师


职位描述

  1. 紧随业界最新的技术动态，并能够结合语音的实际业务场景提出创新的算法及模型；
  2. 负责声学模型的训练和优化等相关工作，深度参与语音合成系统模块的设计、开发，包括文本预处理、分词、词性、韵律、语义理解等模块；
  3. 配合进行产品效率和稳定性优化，解决产品反馈的问题；

职位要求

  1. 计算机、自动化、数学等相关专业硕士及以上学历；
  2. 对数据结构和算法有较好理解，熟练掌握C++ 和 Python；
  3. 有深度学习基础，熟悉常见机器学习、深度学习算法，并对未知算法保有探索好奇心；
  4. 熟悉通用语音合成引擎的搭建及优化，具备引擎优化经验；
  5. 熟悉或了解自然语言处理的常用模型，如BERT、GPT-2等，至少熟练使用一种深度学习框架（PyTorch 或 Tensorflow），并有相关项目经验；
  6. 良好的团队沟通能力，具有优秀的数理逻辑思维能力，对解决挑战性问题充满热情，善于解决问题和分析问题；
  7. 有语音领域相关经历者优先，有发表过语音合成、处理等相关领域的会议论文的优先
